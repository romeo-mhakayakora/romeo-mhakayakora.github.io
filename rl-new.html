"New" Reinforcement Learning (Deep RL & Alignment)

A modern syllabus mapping deep reinforcement learning algorithm mechanics, neural value/policy optimization, and LLM human-preference alignment architectures.

Module 01

Value Function Approximation & DQNs

Deep Q-Networks (DQN), experience replay buffers, target network freezing, and stabilization of off-policy neural function approximation.

Module 02

Policy Gradient Methods

Policy objective functions, Likelihood Ratio estimators, REINFORCE algorithm, policy gradient theorem, and baseline variance reduction techniques.

Module 03

Actor-Critic Algorithms

Advantage Actor-Critic (A2C/A3C), Generalized Advantage Estimation (GAE), baseline-value critics, and parameter sharing.

Module 04

TRPO & Proximal Policy Optimization

Policy optimization bounds, Trust Region Policy Optimization (TRPO), Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO), surrogate loss functions, and clipping heuristics.

Module 05

Model-Based & Exploration Dynamics

Planning with models, Dyna-Q, Monte Carlo Tree Search (MCTS), AlphaGo/AlphaZero architectures, and intrinsic curiosity exploration.

Module 06

Deep RL Alignment & RLHF

Reward modeling, PPO alignment in LLMs, Direct Preference Optimization (DPO), and Process Reward Models (PRMs).
