Architectures & Scope

Modern language modeling spans multiple design regimes. While Large Language Models (LLMs) push the envelope on emergent capabilities and complex reasoning, Small Language Models (SLMs) focus on high-efficiency execution, specialized domains, and deployment on edge devices. My focus is on the training, distillation, and quantization methods that allow compact architectures to retain high cognitive capabilities.
