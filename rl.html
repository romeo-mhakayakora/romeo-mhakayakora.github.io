Reference Guide

Modern Reinforcement Learning

A modern syllabus mapping deep RL algorithm mechanics, neural value/policy optimization, and LLM human-preference alignment architectures.

I write these modules as I learn. Content will be added over time.

Table of contents

  1. Module 01 Value Function Approximation & DQNs Deep Q-Networks (DQN), experience replay buffers, target network freezing, and stabilization of off-policy neural function approximation.
  2. Module 02 Policy Gradient Methods Policy objective functions, Likelihood Ratio estimators, REINFORCE algorithm, policy gradient theorem, and baseline variance reduction techniques.
  3. Module 03 Actor-Critic Algorithms Advantage Actor-Critic (A2C/A3C), Generalized Advantage Estimation (GAE), baseline-value critics, and parameter sharing.
  4. Module 04 TRPO & Proximal Policy Optimization Policy optimization bounds, Trust Region Policy Optimization (TRPO), Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO), surrogate loss functions, and clipping heuristics.
  5. Module 05 Model-Based & Exploration Dynamics Planning with models, Dyna-Q, Monte Carlo Tree Search (MCTS), AlphaGo/AlphaZero architectures, and intrinsic curiosity exploration.
  6. Module 06 Deep RL Alignment & RLHF Reward modeling, PPO alignment in LLMs, Direct Preference Optimization (DPO), and Process Reward Models (PRMs).
