Papers I Read

A repository of academic literature and reviews of foundational computer science papers.

Distributed Systems 2014

In Search of an Understandable Consensus Algorithm (Raft)

Diego Ongaro, John Ousterhout

Introduced the Raft consensus protocol, decomposing replication into leader election, log replication, and safety states to improve understandability over Paxos.

Distributed Systems 2008

Bitcoin: A Peer-to-Peer Electronic Cash System

Satoshi Nakamoto

Constructed a peer-to-peer electronic cash network using a proof-of-work consensus chain to solve the double-spending problem without central authorities.

HPC & Distributed Systems 2004

MapReduce: Simplified Data Processing on Large Clusters

Jeffrey Dean, Sanjay Ghemawat

Designed a programming model and implementation for processing large-scale datasets, automatically handling parallel execution, load balancing, and fault tolerance.
